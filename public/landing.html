Permission ladder

Autonomy you earn and grant.

Every Daemon starts at Level 1. You upgrade autonomy as trust is established.

LEVEL 01 / DEFAULT

Copilot

Reads, summarises, reminds, and suggests. Safe default for every new user.

  • Query company knowledge
  • Summarise decisions and threads
  • Surface blockers and reminders
  • Draft suggestions for review
MOST USED
LEVEL 02 / USER-GRANTED

Assistant

Drafts actions and waits for approval. You stay in the loop on every move.

  • Everything in Level 1
  • Draft tasks and assignments
  • Compose emails for approval
  • Propose calendar changes
LEVEL 03 / USER-GRANTED ONLY

Autonomous

Executes fully and reports back. Full Daemon-to-Daemon workflows. Your explicit grant required.

  • Everything in Level 1 and 2
  • Send emails and schedule meetings
  • Full task handoff pipeline
  • Cross-Daemon coordination
