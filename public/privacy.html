Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

WorkDaemon ("we", "us") provides an AI assistant that connects to the business tools a company chooses to connect. This policy explains what we access, why, and how we protect it.

What we access

Only the data from tools you explicitly connect via OAuth, and only within the scopes you grant. For Slack, that means the channels the WorkDaemon bot is invited to. We never access tools you haven't connected.

How we use it

To power your daemon: answering questions, surfacing relevant findings to the right people, and performing actions you authorize. We do not sell your data or use your company's content to train third-party models.

How we protect it

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM).
  • All traffic is over HTTPS; incoming events are signature-verified.
  • Data is isolated per workspace; one company's data is never exposed to another.

Retention & deletion

Disconnect any integration at any time from Settings → Integrations; we delete its stored tokens immediately. To delete your workspace and associated data, contact us.

Contact

Questions or requests: hello@workdaemon.com.
